~~~~ About Me ~~~~

My name is Hunter Milo Dean, im currently an aspiring web developer, but it wasnt always that way.I grew up as one of many hoodlums of a small bay area city. I enjoyed riding mountain bikes; terrorizing the main street with absolutely ignorant wheelies and a complete disreguard for traffic,fishing and foraging became hobbies of mine the more I spent time outdoors this tied right into my passion for camping or as some might say squating or survivalism. I graduated From Benicia High School in 2017 ready to face the world but with no idea how to do it my skills where theory or only existed the woods. I moved to San Diego to start fresh and

learn in a way i never had before I attened many commuity college courses there, and amassed very few units to show for it. daily expenses started to add up so i came back to the Bay and got to work. I washed dishes, cooked on a line making breakfast and BBQ, I gardened for the aging old ladies around my town next I found myself slicing copious amounts of italian meats in an all-to-arrogant grocery store the cries for oliveloaf-pancetta-procuto-cotto and bloodtounge still ring in my ears I can measure weight within a tenth of a pound by hand (although thats not just from working in a deli...). when these odd jobs couldnt produced what i needed any longer,

I signed on to work the imfamous Comcast. I set up hundreds of modems, routers ethernetswitches, and telephones i realised what i actually wanted to do; it wasnt climbing infastructure poles 10hrs a day or hanging drop cables for someone elses internet service i wanted to work within the electronic ether learn the interworkings of web and hopefully make a living from it, it almost seems oximoronic for a nature fed hoodlum kid to dip his toes into the depths of the Coding pool, but i see it as another frontier to conquer,and since we cant yet get to mars and our soft bodies are to fragile for the depths of the ocean a virtual adventure is what next for me.
